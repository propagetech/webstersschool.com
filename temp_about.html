Our Founder ‚Äî A Life of Purpose

Shri David Jambiah
(24 Feb 1918 to 31 Dec 1989)

A respected educator from Raichur District, Shri David Jambiah served for over four decades as a Principal, School Inspector, teacher, and mentor. His life was guided by two steadfast commitments: Love for God. Love for people.

He founded Websters School with the belief that every child deserves:
  • Quality education.
  • Moral guidance.
  • A caring, disciplined environment.

Under his leadership, Websters grew from 25 students to over 2,300 students in just 13 years ‚Äî a powerful testament to his vision, dedication, and unwavering commitment to education. His legacy continues to guide and inspire everything we do at Websters School.

With well over 4,000 graduates, Websters School is a trusted institution recognised for academic strength, values-based education, and a safe, supportive environment for generations of students.
