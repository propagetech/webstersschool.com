FUTURE DEVELOPMENTS / SUPPORT WEBSTERS

Partner with Us to Shape the Next 50 Years

As Websters School celebrates five decades of learning, we are also looking ahead. A long-term campus development plan is underway to strengthen infrastructure, expand learning spaces, and create future-ready environments for students.

Building the New Websters Campus

Our 10-year development vision focuses on :
  • Modern smart classrooms
  • Expanded playgrounds and sports zones
  • Upgraded science and computer laboratories
  • STEM and Robotics Innovation Centre
  • Arts and music studios
  • Library and digital media centre
  • Green campus initiatives including solar power, rainwater harvesting, and outdoor learning spaces
Why Your Support Matters

Your contribution helps:
  • Create state-of-the-art learning environments.
  • Support deserving students through scholarships.
  • Strengthen sports, arts, and innovation programmes.
  • Enhance student well-being and holistic development.

Every contribution, big or small, directly impacts the future of our students.
