Trusted by families for nearly 50 years

Websters School shapes young minds through strong academics, self-discipline, and values-based learning. We offer education from Pre-Nursery to Class X on our unified campus at Banashankari III Stage, Bengaluru, providing continuity, care, and a nurturing learning environment at every stage.

Why Parents Choose Websters
  • Academic Excellence
    A strong English-medium curriculum aligned with national standards, ensuring academic clarity and confidence.
  • Values & Discipline
    A culture that nurtures self-discipline, respect, leadership, empathy, and responsibility
  • Safe & Caring Environment
    Every child is known, supported, and encouraged in a secure, inclusive campus setting.
  • Holistic Development
    A balanced focus on academics, sports, arts, clubs, and co-curricular learning.
  • A Legacy of Trust
