Secretary

Dr. Ravi David
MA, LLB, PhD
"Joy in Service" is the motto of our school. From humble and small beginnings, over the last four decades, several hundreds of students have completed their school education at Websters and have blossomed to shine as "little flames that burn bright", wherever they have gone.

A team of dedicated staff hasveworked faithfully over decades and continue to be committed to the holistic development of our students-academically, socially, emotionally and ethically. It takes time, effort and perseverance to lay strong foundations upon which a stable superstructure of life can be built.

Our world is rapidly changing and with every change comes new opportunities and new challenges. Amidst the change, we want to ensure that we continue to uphold our culture, heritage and values. We seek to lay foundations that instill in our students both "skillfulness of hands and integrity of heart".

About our staff

Websters School staff have a long-standing reputation for being loyal and committed. They are well trained and have a passion for education. They not only teach students the curriculum but also instill in them values that mould character. The teachers are part of a Professional Development program where ongoing skill assessment and development are routinely conducted.
