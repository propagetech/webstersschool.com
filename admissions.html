Admission Process

1. Enquiry & Campus Visit: Contact us or schedule a guided campus tour.

2 . Application Submission: Apply online or submit the application form at the school office.

3. Submission of Required Documents: Refer to the checklist below.

4. Interaction / Readiness Check: A friendly, age-appropriate interaction for Pre-Primary and select classes.

5. Seat Confirmation: Parents receive official confirmation from the school.

6. Fee Payment & Welcome Kit: Completion of formalities to secure admission.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you offer online admissions?
Yes. Enquiries, application submission, and fee payments can be completed online.

2. Is there an entrance exam?
There is no entrance test for Pre-Primary classes. For higher classes, a basic interaction or assessment helps us understand the child’s learning level.

3. Do you provide student transport?
School transport is available on selected routes. Please contact the admissions team for current route details.

4. Can parents visit the campus before applying?
Absolutely. We encourage parents to visit the campus and interact with our team before taking a decision.
