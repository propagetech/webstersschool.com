Academics

Joyful beginnings. Confident futures.

At Websters School, we believe the earliest years of education lay the foundation for a child’s lifelong learning journey. Our Pre-Nursery and Nursery section (Pre-Nursery to UKG) offers a warm, caring, and stimulating environment where children feel safe, supported, and excited to come to school each day.

Through guided play, exploration, and early language development, children build the essential skills they need to grow with confidence.

Schedule a Campus Visit

Our Approach to Early Learning

We focus on nurturing key developmental areas, including:
  • Language and communication skills
  • Early literacy and numeracy
  • Social confidence and emotional security
  • Creativity and imagination
  • Motor coordination through structured play

Learning happens naturally through stories, music, role-play, outdoor activities, hands-on materials, and movement, allowing children to explore, express, and engage joyfully.

A Space Designed for Young Minds

Our Banashankari campus is thoughtfully designed to meet the needs of early learners, offering:
  • Child-friendly classrooms and restrooms
  • Safe and secure outdoor play areas
  • Caring and attentive staff present throughout the day
  • For more information, please see our Facilities & Student Life page .

Curriculum Snapshot

Children are gently introduced to:
  • English Literacy – phonics, vocabulary, storytelling
  • Numeracy – counting, patterns, and early measurement
  • EVS & General Knowledge – exploring the world around them
  • Art & Craft – colours, shapes, and creative expression
  • Music & Movement – rhythm, coordination, and body awareness
  • Physical Play & Games – strength, balance, and teamwork
  • Bilingual Foundation – gradual introduction to Hindi and Kannada

Every activity reinforces not only concepts but also values such as sharing, patience, cooperation, and respect.

Assessment & Growth Support

Children’s progress is supported through:
  • Continuous, observation-based assessment
  • Age-appropriate periodic evaluations
  • Constructive feedback shared with parents
  • Gentle skill-building for handwriting, conversation, and independence
  • Home-support suggestions to reinforce classroom learning

Our aim is to ensure that every child transitions into Class I with confidence, curiosity, and strong foundational skills.

Strengthening knowledge. Shaping confidence. Inspiring curiosity.

The Primary and Middle School Programme (Classes I–VII) at Websters School builds strong academic foundations while nurturing communication skills, digital literacy, emotional intelligence, and self-discipline. Students learn through interactive lessons, hands-on exploration, and experiences that encourage both clarity of thought and confidence of expression

Strong Foundations for Higher Learning

In Classes I–VII, students are guided to:
  • Strengthen core academic skills
  • Develop independent thinking and problem-solving abilities
  • Learn teamwork and social responsibility
  • Build digital confidence
  • Grow in emotional resilience and self-discipline

We follow the Karnataka State Board curriculum , aligned with the latest NCERT framework and the National Education Policy (NEP). This ensures students are well prepared for competitive examinations and smooth progression to higher classes across national boards.

Teachers provide focused guidance, including remedial support when required, ensuring that every child progresses steadily.

Curriculum Overview

Students study a balanced and comprehensive curriculum that includes:
  • English
  • Kannada (Second Language)
  • Hindi (Third Language)
  • Mathematics
  • General Science
  • Social Studies – History & Geography
  • Computer Science
  • General Knowledge
  • Moral / Value Education
  • Physical Education
Learning is enriched beyond textbooks through:
  • Projects and experiments
  • Audio-visual learning tools
  • Educational field trips
  • Peer collaboration and group work

Learning Facilities

Students benefit from access to:
  • Well-equipped laboratories and a library for practical and independent learning
  • A spacious playground that promotes fitness, coordination, and teamwork

For more details, please visit our Facilities & Student Life page.

Learning Beyond the Classroom

Websters School encourages students to explore interests and talents beyond academics through:
  • Arts and crafts
  • Cultural celebrations and assemblies
  • Clubs including Literary, Science, Humanities, and more
  • Sports such as volleyball, cricket, throwball, kho-kho, and ball badminton
  • Olympiads and academic competitions

Assessment & Feedback

Student progress is monitored through:
  • Daily classroom assessments
  • Concept-reinforcement homework
  • Periodic tests and term examinations
  • Structured report cards
  • Termly Parent-Teacher Meetings

Our goal is to support steady academic growth while ensuring each child’s well-being and confidence

Focused learning. Exam excellence. Future-ready confidence.

The High School Programme (Classes IX–X) at Websters School prepares students for their first major public examination and the academic choices that follow. Our structured, supportive environment ensures strong subject knowledge, disciplined study habits, and emotional resilience.

Our Learning Approach

Students in Classes IX–X benefit from:
  • Concept-oriented teaching that builds clarity and depth
  • Lab-based science learning for practical understanding
  • Exam-focused modules and structured revision cycles
  • Periodic tests and continuous progress tracking
  • Personalised academic feedback
  • Remedial support and special coaching where required

Teachers work closely with students to strengthen fundamentals and build confidence for board examinations.

Curriculum Overview

Languages & Humanities
  • English
  • Kannada / Hindi
  • Social Science – History, Civics, Geography, and Economics
  • Value Education
  • Science & Mathematics
  • Mathematics – Algebra, Arithmetic, and Geometry
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology

Additional Learning
  • Computer Science
  • Physical Education
  • SUPW (Socially Useful Productive Work)

This balanced curriculum provides a strong foundation for PUC, diploma programmes, and national curriculum pathways.

Learning Environment

High school students learn in an environment that supports both academic focus and overall development, with access to:
  • Science and computer laboratories for hands-on learning
  • Library resources for extended reading and research
  • Sports facilities to stay active and develop teamwork

More information is available on our Facilities & Student Life page.

Life Beyond Academics

To support holistic growth, students participate in:
  • Literary, Humanities, Science, and Music clubs
  • Cultural events, exhibitions, and talent showcases
  • Sports coaching and inter-school competitions
  • Community service initiatives
  • Leadership roles and student council activities

These experiences help shape communication skills, responsibility, and character.

Career & Counselling Support

A full-time trained counsellor supports students through:
  • Academic planning and effective study strategies
  • Emotional well-being and stress management
  • Workshops and guidance sessions
  • Support in planning high school to higher education or career pathways

Assessment for Continuous Improvement

Student progress is reviewed through:
  • In-class concept checks
  • Periodic tests and mock board examinations
  • Individual progress reports
  • Termly Parent-Teacher Meetings

Our aim is steady improvement, reduced exam-related stress, and strong board outcomes.
