STUDENT LIFE & FACILITIES

A safe, modern, and inspiring environment for learning, growth, and exploration

Websters School offers a thoughtfully designed campus that supports academic excellence, creativity, physical development, and emotional well-being. Our facilities and student programmes work together to create a balanced and enriching school experience.

Sports & Physical Development

Physical education and sports play an important role in student development at Websters School. Facilities and programmes include:
  • A large, secure playground
  • Volleyball, throwball, kho-kho, ball badminton, cricket, and kabaddi
  • Regular Mass PT sessions
  • Participation in inter-school and district-level sports events
  • Trained Physical Education instructors
  • An Annual Sports Day featuring the school’s Brass Band

Academic Facilities

Smart Classrooms
Bright, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with audio-visual tools that support interactive and engaging lessons.

Science Laboratories
Dedicated Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories that encourage hands-on experimentation and scientific thinking.

Computer Laboratory
A fully equipped computer lab with individual systems and high-speed internet access, supporting digital literacy from an early age.

Audio-Visual Learning Room
An AV room that enhances classroom teaching through educational videos, presentations, and demonstrations.

Library
A well-stocked library with books, journals, and reference materials that encourage reading, research, and independent learning.

Extra-curricular Activities

Creative & Cultural Facilities
Students are encouraged to express themselves creatively through:
  • Art and craft spaces
  • Music Club sessions (vocals and guitar)
  • Cultural festivals, exhibitions, and stage performances
These activities help nurture confidence, creativity, and self-expression.

Clubs & Student Engagement
Clubs at Websters School provide opportunities for exploration beyond academics:
  • Science Club – models, experiments, and science exhibitions
  • Humanities Club – drama, rangoli, storytelling, and creative expression
  • Literary Club – poetry, prose, and debates in English, Kannada, and Hindi
  • Music Club – basic vocal and guitar training
Scouts & Guides
are being reintroduced to build leadership, discipline, teamwork, and community responsibility.

Events & Celebrations
The school calendar includes a range of events that celebrate learning, culture, and values:
  • Annual David Jambaiah Memorial Debate & Quiz
  • Pre-Primary celebrations such as Red Day, Blue Day, Green Day, Show & Tell, and Fancy Dress
  • National and state celebrations including Republic Day, Independence Day, and Karnataka Rajyotsava


Transport Facilities

Websters School provides school-operated transport services with a focus on safety and reliability:
  • Fleet of school buses
  • Compliance with all statutory safety regulations
  • Attendant on every route
  • Enrolment on a first-come, first-served basis
  • Reasonable transport fees


Counselling & Student Wellness

Student well-being is supported by a full-time qualified counsellor, who provides:
  • Emotional and psychological support
  • Academic planning and guidance
  • Workshops on exam preparation, stress management, and well-being
  • Support for parents and teachers when required
