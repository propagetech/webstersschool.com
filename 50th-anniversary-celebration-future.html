50 Years Celebration

Honouring the Past. Celebrating the Present. Inspiring the Future.

ðŸ“… Save the Date: 20th & 21st February 2026

For five decades, Websters School has stood as a place where education goes beyond classrooms and learning is shaped by values, discipline, and care. What began in 1976 with just 25 students has grown into a trusted institution that has nurtured thousands of learners across generations.

The Golden Jubilee is not just a milestone. It is a celebration of every student, parent, teacher, staff member, and alumnus who has been part of the Websters journey.

Celebrating a Legacy of Learning Since 1976

Over the last 50 years, Websters School has:

  • Built strong academic foundations rooted in values
  • Shaped character alongside intellect
  • Created a safe and nurturing environment for generations of students

This legacy continues to guide us as we look towards the future.
